1 Introduction

Welcome to SevaLink Partner. SevaLink Technologies Private Limited ("SevaLink," "we," "our," or "us") operates the SevaLink Partner mobile application ("SevaLink Partner") designed for drivers, paramedics, nurses, and other healthcare service providers who fulfill service bookings on the SevaLink platform.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, process, use, store, share, and protect your personal data when you register as a service provider and use SevaLink Partner. It is separate from the SevaLink Privacy Policy that applies to end-users booking healthcare services.

By registering as a driver/provider and using SevaLink Partner, you consent to the data practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use SevaLink Partner.

🚗 This policy specifically covers SevaLink Partner. SevaLink Partner collects additional data types (such as background location and KYC documents) compared to SevaLink, due to the nature of providing on-demand service fulfillment.

2 Information We Collect

A. Identity & Profile Information

  • Full legal name, date of birth, gender, and profile photograph.
  • Mobile number, email address, and home/operating address.
  • Emergency contact details.

B. KYC & Professional Verification Documents

  • Government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN card, Passport, Driving License).
  • Vehicle registration certificate (RC), vehicle insurance, and vehicle fitness certificate (for drivers).
  • Professional license/certificate (e.g., nursing registration, paramedic certification) for healthcare providers.
  • Police verification certificate.
  • Bank account details for earnings disbursement (Account number, IFSC code).

C. Location Data (Critical)

  • Real-time GPS location: Collected continuously while you are "online" (available to accept bookings) in the Driver App, both in the foreground and background.
  • Trip tracking: Start point, route, and end point for every service trip you fulfill.
  • Idle location: General area when you are online but not on an active trip, used to optimize dispatch.

⚠️ Background Location Notice: SevaLink Partner requires background location access to track your position while you are online, even when the app is minimized. This is essential for the dispatch system to assign you nearby bookings. You can stop all tracking by going "Offline" within the app or revoking location permission in device settings.

D. Service Performance Data

  • Booking acceptance rate, cancellation rate, and average response time.
  • Trip completion records, duration, and distance traveled.
  • Customer ratings and feedback received.
  • Incidents or complaints linked to your account.

E. Device & Technical Data

  • Device model, OS version, app version, and device identifiers.
  • Battery level and network connectivity status (to assess location reporting reliability).
  • Push notification tokens (FCM) for real-time booking alerts.
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics.

4 Sharing of Information

Your data is shared only with parties necessary to operate the platform:

  • Users (Patients): Your first name, profile photo, vehicle details (for ambulance/transport), and real-time location are shared with the patient once a booking is assigned to you, to enable tracking and communication.
  • Payment Processor (Cashfree): Bank account details and earning summaries are shared for salary/commission disbursement. Cashfree complies with RBI regulations and PCI-DSS standards.
  • KYC Verification Partner: Identity documents may be shared with a licensed KYC verification provider for automated document validation.
  • Cloud Infrastructure (AWS): All data is stored on AWS India (ap-south-1 region) under strict data processing agreements.
  • Google Firebase: Device tokens shared solely for push notification delivery.
  • Regulatory & Legal Authorities: Trip records, location history, and identity documents may be disclosed to law enforcement, transport authorities, or healthcare regulators when legally required.
  • Insurance Companies: Trip and location data may be shared in the event of an accident or incident to facilitate insurance claims, only with your knowledge unless legally required otherwise.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5 Location & Background Tracking

SevaLink Partner uses location data more extensively than SevaLink due to the real-time nature of dispatch. Here is a detailed breakdown:

ScenarioLocation AccessPurpose
App open, status "Offline" Not collected No tracking when offline
App open, status "Online" Foreground + Background (every 30 seconds or 50m movement) Dispatch optimization, nearest provider matching
Active trip in progress High-frequency (every 10 seconds) Real-time navigation, patient tracking view
App closed Background (when Online status active) Maintain dispatch availability

Location data from completed trips is retained for 90 days in raw form, then aggregated and anonymized for analytics. You can stop all location collection by tapping "Go Offline" in the app or revoking location permissions in device settings.

📱 Android users will see a persistent notification while background location tracking is active. This notification is required by Android OS for transparency and cannot be disabled independently of the tracking itself.

6 KYC & Document Verification

To operate on the SevaLink platform, you are required to submit identity and professional documents. We handle these documents with extreme care:

  • Documents are uploaded over encrypted connections (TLS 1.2+) and stored in encrypted AWS S3 buckets with restricted access.
  • Access to KYC documents is limited to authorized SevaLink compliance staff and our KYC verification partner.
  • Aadhaar numbers are masked after verification; we only retain the last 4 digits of your Aadhaar in our system.
  • Documents are retained for the duration of your active partnership + 5 years thereafter, as required by applicable RBI/SEBI/transport regulations for records of contracted workers.
  • You may request a copy of your documents from us at any time by writing to privacy@sevalink.in.

7 Earnings & Financial Data

As a service provider on SevaLink, your earnings are calculated based on completed trips, applicable commission rates, incentives, and any penalties per the SevaLink Partner Agreement.

  • Bank account details provided for payout are encrypted and stored securely; only truncated account numbers are displayed in the app.
  • Payout records are processed through Cashfree Payments and are subject to their privacy practices and RBI regulations.
  • Earnings records, commission calculations, and TDS deduction certificates (Form 16A) are maintained for 7 years for statutory compliance under Indian income tax regulations.
  • You will receive a financial summary accessible in the Driver App under "Earnings" and via email statements.

9 Data Security

Given the sensitive nature of driver data (including location, identity documents, and financial records), we implement stringent security controls:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit between the Driver App and our servers.
  • AES-256 encryption for all sensitive data at rest including KYC documents and financial records.
  • Private S3 buckets with pre-signed URL access for document retrieval — documents are never publicly accessible.
  • JWT-based authentication with short-lived access tokens and refresh token rotation.
  • Redis-backed session management with automatic TTL expiry to limit session hijacking exposure.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) across all internal admin systems.
  • Anomaly detection: Unusual login patterns or location anomalies trigger automatic alerts.
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits by third-party specialists.

In the event of a security incident, we will notify affected individuals within 72 hours via in-app notification and email, and report to relevant authorities as required by law.

10 Your Rights

As a data principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you, including trip records and KYC documents.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data (e.g., incorrect bank details, document errors).
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data after partnership termination (subject to mandatory legal retention periods).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for non-essential data uses (e.g., promotional communications). Note: Withdrawing consent for essential functions like location tracking will affect your ability to use the Driver App.
  • Right to Portability: Request your trip history and earnings data in a machine-readable format (CSV).
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer within the App or via email.
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate someone to exercise your data rights in the event of incapacity or death.

You can manage notification preferences and view your data via Driver App Settings → Privacy. For all other requests, email privacy@sevalink.in. We will respond within 30 days.

11 Children's Privacy

SevaLink Partner is exclusively intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. As a prerequisite, all service providers must possess valid government-issued identification, professional licenses, and contractual capacity, which require being a legal adult.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If we become aware that a minor has registered on the platform, we will immediately deactivate that account and delete associated data.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. For material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date;
  • Send a push notification and/or email to all active drivers;
  • Display an in-app banner requiring acknowledgment of the updated policy before continued use.

Changes affecting how we process your location data or KYC documents will always require your explicit re-consent before taking effect. Your continued use of the Driver App constitutes acceptance of non-material updates.
