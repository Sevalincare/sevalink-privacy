1 Introduction

Welcome to SevaLink. SevaLink Technologies Private Limited ("SevaLink," "we," "our," or "us") operates the SevaLink mobile application ("SevaLink App" or "App") and the website at sevalink.in (collectively, the "Platform").

We provide on-demand healthcare services including ambulance booking, home healthcare, teleconsultation, and ancillary medical services to users across India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, use, store, share, and protect your personal data when you access or use our Platform.

By downloading, registering for, or using the SevaLink App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the App.

📋 This policy applies specifically to the SevaLink User App (available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store). If you are a driver or service provider, please refer to the SevaLink Driver App Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Full name, mobile number, email address, date of birth, gender, and profile photograph.
  • Patient Profiles: Medical history notes, allergy information, emergency contacts, and health conditions you voluntarily add for yourself or family members.
  • Booking Details: Service type, preferred date/time, special instructions, and the address where the service is required.
  • Payment Information: UPI ID, saved card details (tokenized via our payment processor), and billing address.
  • Communications: Messages sent to customer support, feedback, and app reviews.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (Android ID, IDFV), app version, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, booking history, session duration, and crash logs.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS coordinates (when you grant location permission) to dispatch the nearest available service provider.
  • Log Data: IP address, browser/app type, timestamps, referral URLs, and diagnostic data.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Used on our website for session management and analytics. The App uses equivalent local storage mechanisms.

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Google / Apple Sign-In: Name, email, and profile picture if you register via social login.
  • Payment Processors (Cashfree): Transaction status, payment instrument type, and masked card information.
  • Referral Partners: If you were referred to SevaLink via a partner, we may receive basic contact information to credit the referral.

4 Sharing of Information

We share your information only as described below and never for purposes incompatible with those stated in this policy.

  • Service Providers (Drivers & Healthcare Partners): Your name, contact number, and service address are shared with the assigned provider to fulfill your booking. Providers are contractually bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Payment Processors (Cashfree Payments): Financial transaction data is shared to process payments securely. Cashfree maintains its own privacy policy compliant with PCI-DSS standards.
  • Cloud Infrastructure (AWS): Your data is stored on AWS servers located in India (ap-south-1 region). AWS acts as a data processor under strict data processing agreements.
  • Push Notification Services (Firebase Cloud Messaging): Device tokens are shared with Google Firebase solely to deliver booking status notifications.
  • Analytics (Internal): Aggregated and anonymized usage statistics used for product improvement.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights and safety of SevaLink, its users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. You will be notified via email or prominent notice on our App before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

5 Location Data

Location access is essential to SevaLink's core functionality. Here is how we handle it:

  • Foreground Location: Used when the App is actively open to dispatch the nearest available provider and to display your position on the map during an active booking.
  • Address Entry: You may also manually type your service address if you prefer not to share precise location data.
  • No Background Tracking: The SevaLink User App does not track your location in the background or when the app is closed.

📍 You can revoke location permission at any time via your device settings (Settings → Apps → SevaLink → Permissions). Note that revoking location access may limit the functionality of the App.

6 Health & Medical Data

Healthcare data is treated with the highest level of sensitivity. Any medical information you provide (allergies, conditions, medical history) is:

  • Collected only with your explicit consent;
  • Shared only with the healthcare provider assigned to your booking and only to the extent necessary to deliver the service;
  • Stored in encrypted form with access controls limiting which staff can view identifiable health records;
  • Never used for profiling, advertising, or sold to any third party including pharmaceutical or insurance companies;
  • Subject to retention limits — health records are retained for a minimum of 3 years as required under applicable Indian medical records regulations, after which they are securely deleted.

Our practices are designed to align with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and guidelines issued by the National Digital Health Mission (NDHM).

7 Payments & Financial Data

All payment processing is handled by Cashfree Payments India Private Limited, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment aggregator regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).

  • SevaLink does not store full card numbers on its servers.
  • Card information is tokenized by Cashfree and the token is stored for repeat payment convenience.
  • UPI transactions are processed directly through the NPCI network via Cashfree.
  • All payment pages use TLS 1.2+ encryption.

You can manage your saved payment methods at any time from the App's "Payment Methods" settings screen.

9 Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest on AWS RDS and S3.
  • Access controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures only authorized personnel can access personal data.
  • Secure infrastructure: Our backend runs on AWS with VPC isolation, private subnets, and Security Groups.
  • Vulnerability management: Regular security audits and dependency scanning.
  • Incident response: We maintain an incident response plan. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

While we strive to protect your data, no system is completely immune from risk. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your app updated.

10 Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and other applicable laws, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing activities based on consent (e.g., marketing communications).
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see Section 13).
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate an individual to exercise your data rights in the event of your incapacity or death.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@sevalink.in. We will respond within 30 days. You can also manage many preferences directly from the App Settings → Privacy.

11 Children's Privacy

The SevaLink App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 18 without verifiable parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your minor child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@sevalink.in and we will take prompt steps to delete that information.

When a user creates a dependent/family member profile for a minor, the account-holding adult is responsible for all consents on behalf of the child.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page;
  • Send you a push notification or email notification (for significant changes); and
  • Display a prominent notice within the App.

Your continued use of the App after the effective date of any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
